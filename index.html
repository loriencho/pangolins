The Pangolin

The very trait with the purpose of protecting pangolins has led to the species' endangerment: its tough, protective scales.

About

The pangolin, sometimes called the "scaly anteater," is an animal only the size of an average housecat! There are eight different pangolin species: four that live in Africa, and another four that live in Asia. According to the International Union for the Conservation of Nature (IUCN), there's almost no information available on population levels of any species of pangolin, but they are all thought to be in decline.
